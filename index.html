16th July 2021

Mallow STAR

Corkman wrestled to the ground

For some it is another day here in Ballyhaunis. Most people go about their business while some are still forced to stay at home due to ongoing restrictions. For some though it is yet another day where in the line of duty the local Gardai were called to an unruly incident. Flor Corcoran, a 60 year old mechanic from Cork was wrestled to the ground from the top of a John Deere 7750 silage harvester. One on-looker Albert Madden reports that this type of behaviour is a common occurrence by said Mr. Corcoran. Mr. Brian Higgins, the owner of the harvester was not amused by this outlandish behaviour which was witnessed by impressionable teenagers. Later that day he was spotted in someones yard performing weird rituals. One of the residents from the house, where this activity took action, offered him some water which Mr. Corcoran angrily refused.

A recovering Mr. Corcoron showed no remorse the following day for his immature behaviour for a man of his vintage. The matter has been deferred on the agreement that Mr. Corcoran attend a counsellor for his addictive behaviour.
